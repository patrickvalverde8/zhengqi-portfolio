About me

I am a graduate student at Tsinghua University, specializing in Urban Human Factors Engineering and AI‑driven design systems. My work explores how artificial intelligence, data science, and human experience converge to build intelligent environments and creative tools that understand people — not just data.

As the Co‑founder & COO of AlloTech, an AI platform for generative manufacturing design, I lead cross‑functional teams combining algorithmic modeling, data engineering, and product design. My broader research investigates how human factors principles can shape the next generation of AI‑driven urban and digital systems.

My journey moves between research, entrepreneurship, and design — from conducting large‑scale data analysis at ByteDance and Meituan to leading projects that merge machine learning with spatial cognition. Outside the studio, I find balance in mountain hiking and outdoor exploration, where I draw inspiration for resilient design thinking.
